About OASIS

By adopting FOSS, organizations in the social sector can leverage powerful tools, reduce costs, and contribute to a collaborative ecosystem of technological innovation.

Mission

To address systemic technological gaps and lack of technological capacity in the social sector through the adoption of FOSS.

To help organizations attain technological self-sufficiency to better focus on their social work and increase its impact.

To help create a thriving ecosystem of FOSS innovation and collaboration in the social sector, made of diverse stakeholders.
